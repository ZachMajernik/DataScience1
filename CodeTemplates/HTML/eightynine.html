Lets look at just 1989, the launch of Billboards "Hot Rap Singles" chart:

In 1989, regional independent labels dominated with superstar signings: Young MC and the Pharcyde are signed to Delicious Vinyl. Biz Markie (Just a Friend), Big Daddy Kane (Smooth Operator), and Marley Marl are all on Cold Chillin'. Ruthless, Easy E's label, has N.W.A and the D.O.C on it's roster. Jive is crushing it with a roster that includes Boogie Down Productions. DJ Jazzy Jeff and the Fresh Prince, and Too Short. And Tommy Boy Presciently released both De La Soul and Queen Latifah's first albums.
